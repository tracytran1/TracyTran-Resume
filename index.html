My dream is to become a teacher for low-income community schools because they deserve good education. I want to be a teacher because I believe that all students should have proper education. Being a teacher is more than teaching a subject. I want to work together with students to meet to help reach their goals in the future. I have been a tutor at Kumon. At Kumon, I tutored elementary students in my senior year and it was fulfilling to help students understand different school subjects. To add on, I was in a club called Motivating Matadors in my sophomore year. This club allowed me to assist the freshmen in my high school with the different events and academic classes. I was able to grow communication skills with the freshmen and gain more experience with educating freshmen to a highschool system.

Conflict Resolution was a highlight in highschool because I was able to give back to my community. I was taught the effiecient way to show empathy in a way that people feel validated and comfortable to speak about how they are feeling. This was a helpfulway to reach my goal as a teacher because teachers need to be understanding of their students.
